SignalShield Analyst is a crypto intelligence agent integrating Gemini and CoinGecko.  
It supports hype, sentiment, riskcheck, trend, alerts, market data, and AI reasoning.  
Fully compatible with Teneo agent lifecycle and WebSocket communication.

Commands tested:
- hype <token>
- sentiment <token>
- riskcheck <token>
- gecko <token>
- ai "<prompt>"
- alert <token> "<condition>"

Built in Go, using Gemini v1beta API, CoinGecko price feed, and mock/live scanners.
